3D Egg and Pyramid created with use of OpenGL and GLUT
This project was made by following instructions on the Wroc³aw University of Science and Technology website http://www.zsk.ict.pwr.wroc.pl/zsk/dyd/intinz/gk/lab/

Controls:
Left Mouse Button - rotate the object
Right Mouse Button - zoom in and out
w - change to egg
p - change to pyramid

While in pyramid:
1-5 - turn walls visible/invisible
6 - turn cull_face on/off
